Project #7 Hamster

Description

This is a big project. It's a Hamster game that I remade. There are three scenes in total. The second scene is a game. When you have played enough, you can press the space to go to the third scene to see how many points you have scored.

Design Process

There are three scenes in this game. The first scene is the font design for an early game, the second scene is the game, and the third scene is the final score. The position of the game content is used to detect the collision, and then transmitted to the scoreboard.
